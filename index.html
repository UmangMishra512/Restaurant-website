Our Story

Welcome to The Sky Cafe, a hidden gem perched above the bustling streets of Patna. Born from a passion for great food and memorable evenings, we have crafted a space where every visit feels special.

Whether you're looking for a romantic dining experience under the stars, a vibrant spot for friends gatherings, or a family-friendly atmosphere to celebrate life's moments, our premium rooftop setting delivers it all.

We pride ourselves on offering a luxurious experience at prices that bring a smile. Join us for your next birthday celebration or a casual evening out.
